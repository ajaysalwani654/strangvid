Common Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

No account is needed at all. StrangerLink is completely anonymous and registration-free. Just open the website, choose your chat mode, and you're instantly connected to a stranger. We never ask for your name, email, phone number, or any personal information.
Yes, 100% free — always. There are no premium plans, no coins to buy, no hidden fees, and no subscription. Every feature including video chat, text chat, interest matching, and language filters is available to everyone at no cost.
StrangerLink uses WebRTC (Web Real-Time Communication) technology, which is built directly into modern browsers. This means your video and audio are transmitted peer-to-peer between you and your chat partner — encrypted, low-latency, and without needing any plugins or app downloads. It works on Chrome, Firefox, Edge, and Safari.
Absolutely. StrangerLink is fully mobile-responsive and works on all smartphones and tablets via the browser. Both video chat and text chat work on mobile. For video chat, you can use either your front or rear camera. No app download is required — just open strangerlink.com in your mobile browser.
Interest Tags allow you to enter up to 5 topics you're interested in (for example: "music", "gaming", "travel", "coding", "cricket"). Our matching algorithm then tries to pair you with strangers who have added the same or similar tags. If no match is found quickly, you'll still be paired with a random user so you're never left waiting long.
Spy Mode is a unique three-way format. One user (the "spy") submits a question and then watches as two strangers discuss it in a live chat — without being able to type themselves. The two strangers know someone is watching but don't know who. The spy can disconnect at any time. It's a fun way to see how others think about different topics.
No conversations are stored on our servers. Video streams are peer-to-peer (via WebRTC) and are not routed through or recorded by StrangerLink. Text chat messages are only held temporarily in memory to facilitate the live session and are discarded once the chat ends. You can optionally save your own text chat log locally using the "Save Log" button, but this never leaves your device.
Click the red "Report" button immediately visible during any chat. You can then select the reason (nudity/NSFW, harassment, hate speech, spam, underage user, or other) and submit. You can also click "Skip" or close the chat window at any time to disconnect instantly. Our moderation team reviews all reports and takes action — including permanent bans — usually within 24 hours.
Yes. StrangerLink is strictly for users aged 18 and above. By using the platform you confirm that you are at least 18 years old. We use AI-assisted age estimation tools and rely on user reports to detect and remove underage users. If you believe someone is underage, please report them immediately using the Report button.
The platform interface is available in English, Hindi, Spanish, French, German, Portuguese, Arabic, Turkish, Japanese, Korean, and 40+ more languages. You can also set a language preference in the Language Filter so you are matched with strangers who speak your preferred language. This is especially useful for language learners wanting to practice with native speakers.
